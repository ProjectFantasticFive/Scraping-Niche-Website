OUR PROJECTS

Private Classical Guitar

Consists of 4 levels. For elementary guitar lessons, there is an understanding that the guitar beam is not of shape, no value, how to read, and apply it to the outside. Use a nylon guitar and play common classical songs.The target of the learning process for elementary guitar lessons is 6 months (from more than 70% to less than 6 months). Sometimes with our learning methods, not a few students can finish faster, this really depends on the seriousness, diligence and enthusiasm for learning.Assistance and discipline to learn from you / parents greatly help this process.


Private Fingerstyle Guitar

Private Guitar Fingerstyle / acoustics for the basis provided is not a guitar tabulation / tablature / TAB, no value, how to read, how to pick, guitar chords and their development. Use nylon guitar or stringed acoustic guitar and usually play songs that are Hit, children's songs, pop / rock public or you need the song you want with our guitar teacher. For those of you who have a fairly good level of play, you can also use guitar lessons in the blues / jazz genre and we are ready to serve you.

For those of you who choose a hobby class the learning process is 4 months. Not a few of our students survive faster.It really depends on the seriousness of your choice of material, diligence and enthusiasm for learning are crucial. Target according to music school standards is 6 months (more appropriate material).


Private Electric Guitar

Private electric guitar lessons for bases containing tabulations / tablature / TAB, scales on the guitar, introducing modes and their development, learning to make melodies, and understanding chord formulas. For all genres of pop, rock, blues, jazz, and others
